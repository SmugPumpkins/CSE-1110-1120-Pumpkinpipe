# Challenge 07 - Finger Painting

The purpose of this challenge is to practice placing and keeping drawn elements on the screen after they have been added.

## Mild 🌶️

Create a program that meets the following criteria:

* A live video feed is captured throug a webcam.
* Hands are detected.
* Hands are drawn.
* When the thumb and index tips are close together, the program draws lines following the x and y paths of the tip of the index finger (like drawing on the screen)

## Medium 🌶️🌶️

Create a program that meets the following criteria:

* A live video feed is captured throug a webcam.
* Hands are detected.
* Hands are drawn.
* When the thumb and index tips are close together, the program draws lines following the x and y paths of the tip of the index finger (like drawing on the screen)
* The bottom of the screen has at least 5 rectangles filled in with different colors.
* When the index finger overlaps with one of the colors, then the color of the line being drawn gets changed to match that color.

## Spicy 🌶️🌶️🌶️

Create a program that meets the following criteria:

* A live video feed is captured throug a webcam.
* Hands are detected.
* Hands are drawn.
* When the thumb and index tips are close together, the program draws lines following the x and y paths of the tip of the index finger (like drawing on the screen)
* The bottom of the screen has at least 5 rectangles filled in with different colors.
* When the index finger overlaps with one of the colors, then the color of the line being drawn gets changed to match that color.
* Add functionality so that left hands and right hands can have different line colors, and draw simultaneously as separate hands without interfereing with one another.
