# Challenge 10 - Whack a Mole

## Mild 🌶️

Create a program that meets the following criteria:

* A live video feed is captured throug a webcam.
* Hands are detected.
* Hands are drawn.
* Circles (or rectangles or some other shape) that represent moles are placed at random positions on the screen at random intervals of time.
* There is text in the top left corner of the screen to display the human player's score.
* When the tip of the index finger overlaps a "mole", the mole is deleted and the score is increased by 1.
* The game continues forever.

## Medium 🌶️🌶️

Create a program that meets the following criteria:

* A live video feed is captured throug a webcam.
* Hands are detected.
* Hands are drawn.
* Circles (or rectangles or some other shape) that represent moles are placed at random positions on the screen at random intervals of time.
* There is text in the top left corner of the screen to display the human player's score.
* Above the current score there is the high score for the current session.
* When the tip of the index finger overlaps a "mole", the mole is deleted and the score is increased by 1.
* There is a timer in the top right corner of the screen to show how much linger the player has until the game ends.
* When the timer ends, the score for session is saved if it is the highest score for the session.
* When the timer ends, the player is presented with 2 rectangles that they can overlap to either close the game or play again.

Create a program that meets the following criteria:

* There is a timer in the top right corner of the screen to show how much linger the player has until the game ends.
* When the timer ends, the score for session is saved if it is the highest score of all time.
* When the timer ends, the player is presented with 2 rectangles that they can overlap to either close the game or play again.
