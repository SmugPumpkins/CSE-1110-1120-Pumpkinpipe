# Challenge 03 - Tap Detection

The purpose of this challenge is to practice dealing with event loop timing as well as landmark distances on the hands.

## Mild 🌶️

Create a program that meets the following criteria:

* A live video feed is captured through a webcam.
* Hands are detected.
* Hands are drawn.
* When the index fingertip and the thumb tip are put together, the word "tap" is displayed as text on the screen.

![Challenge 03 Mild](./Images/challenge_03_mild.gif)

## Medium 🌶️🌶️

Create a program that meets the following criteria:

* A live video feed is captured through a webcam.
* Hands are detected.
* Hands are drawn.
* When any fingertip and the thumb tip are put together, the word "tap" is displayed as text on the screen with the name of the finger being tapped (for example, `index`, `middle`, `ring`, or `pinky`).

![Challenge 03 Medium](./Images/challenge_03_medium.gif)

## Spicy 🌶️🌶️🌶️

Create a program that meets the following criteria:

* A live video feed is captured through a webcam.
* Hands are detected.
* Hands are drawn.
* A counter starting at 0 is displayed in the top left corner of the screen.
* Whenever the thumb and the index finger are first touched together, the counter increases by 1.
* The counter does not increase while the fingers are held together, only on a new "tap".
* *Hint: If your counter is feeling like it's skipping, try adding a "dead zone"!*
